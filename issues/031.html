News
Welcome Gareth Stockdale as the new Micro:bit Foundation CEO
Gareth joins from BBC Learning where he was joint lead for the original project that designed, developed and deployed the micro:bit to all year 7 or equivalent children in the UK, subsequently joining the Foundation board.
New Product: Klip Halo for the BBC micro:bit
Breaks out all of the pins from the micro:bit to pads spaced around the edge of the Klip Halo. The pads have been carefully designed and spaced so that they are ideal for use with E-Textiles projects and also for projects that require the use of Crocodile Leads.
New Product: Bit:Commander
The Bit:Commander is a Console & Controller for the BBC micro:bit and a great device for powering and experimenting.
