News

Farnell element14 extends BBC Micro:bit availability in Europe
In response to an unprecedented demand following the gifting of one million BBC micro:bits to schools across the UK, Farnell element14 has broadened availability of the BBC micro:bit to resellers across Europe.

Acrylic case for micro:bit - choose your colour
New product from Proto-Pic, a colourful laser cut acrylic cases to protect your micro:bit while still allowing full access to the edge connector.
 

Articles

BBC micro:bit Touch pHAT CAP1166
Using this Raspberry Pi board with 6 capacitive touch sensors and 6 LEDs with the micro:bit.

Driving DotStar APA102 LED strings with the BBC micro:bit
Driving APA102 and APA102C LEDs with MicroPython via SPI.

What is boolean logic? KS3 discovery lesson resources using BBC micro:bit
Teaching resource for pupils in key stage 3 (aged 11-14) to understand simple Boolean logic and some of its uses in circuits and programming.

Big Button Project Prototype
Demo connecting micro:bits to a Linux single board computer using Bluetooth Low Energy.
