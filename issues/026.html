Hi everyone,
If you are familiar with PyCon you'll already be aware of how awesome and welcoming it is, and this year PyCon UK (26th to 30th of October in Cardiff) will get even awesomer!
For the first time ever, it will include a micro:bit mini summit on the 29th of October, an unmissable event to join the Foundation and the micro:bit community in a day full of fantastic talks and activities.
Tickets and accommodation will run out quickly, so do not hesitate in join us on this wonderful celebration!
 
 

News
micro:bit mini Summit at PyCon UK
This year for the first time, we’re having a micro:bit mini Summit as part of PyCon UK, a conference that has always had a special place in micro:bit history.
micro:bit Launches in India
If you're in India and you've been waiting to get your hands on the BBC micro:bit, we have some very good news for you... it will be officially launched in Mumbai on Tuesday, 19th September at DIDAC INDIA!
New Book: Getting Started with the micro:bit
New book from Make by Wolfram Donat. Getting Started with the micro:bit - Coding and Making with the BBC's Open Development Board.
New Product: ZIP Halo
Add some colour to your next coding project with our new Halo board for the BBC micro:bit. The Halo has 24 ZIP LEDs, which are individually addressable full colour LEDs. This means that each LED can display a huge spectrum of colours, allowing amazing colourful effects to be achieved.
